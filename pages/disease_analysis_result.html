Bacterial infection requiring antibiotic treatment. See a healthcare provider for proper diagnosis and treatment.

Common Symptoms:
  • • Severe sore throat
  • • Red, swollen tonsils
  • • White patches on throat
  • • Fever above 101°F
  • • Swollen lymph nodes
Duration:

3-7 days with treatment

When to See a Doctor:

Immediately for proper diagnosis and antibiotic treatment

Urgency Level:

Moderate - Schedule appointment within 24-48 hours

Treatment & Cure Information
Prescription Medications:
  • • Amoxicillin (first-line antibiotic)
  • • Penicillin VK (alternative)
  • • Azithromycin (if allergic to penicillin)
  • • Pain relievers (acetaminophen, ibuprofen)
Home Care & Relief:
  • • Warm salt water gargles
  • • Stay hydrated with warm liquids
  • • Use throat lozenges or sprays
  • • Get plenty of rest
  • • Use a humidifier
Treatment Duration & Important Notes:

Antibiotic Course: Complete the full 7-10 day course even if symptoms improve

Recovery Time: Most patients feel better within 24-48 hours of starting antibiotics

⚠️ Important: Return to doctor if symptoms worsen or don't improve within 3 days of treatment

Recommended Healthcare Providers & Departments
Primary Care Options:
  • Family Medicine - General diagnosis and antibiotic treatment
  • Internal Medicine - Adult care and complications management
  • Urgent Care Centers - Quick diagnosis and treatment
Specialist Referrals (If Needed):
  • ENT (Otolaryngology) - For recurrent or complicated cases
  • Infectious Disease - For resistant infections
  • Pediatrics - For children under 18
